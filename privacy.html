Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

CallsCoach collects the following information:

  • Google Account Information: Name, email address, and profile picture when you sign in with Google
  • Call Data: Transcripts, call duration, platform used, and call timestamps
  • Usage Data: Number of calls, coaching preferences, and settings

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide access to the service
  • Provide real-time AI coaching during calls
  • Generate call summaries and notes
  • Improve our AI coaching algorithms
  • Track usage and maintain service quality

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely in Google Firebase with encryption
  • Audio is NOT recorded - only text transcripts are stored
  • We use industry-standard security practices
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with third parties except:

  • OpenAI API (for AI processing - they do not store data)
  • When required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (by not using the service)

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the service. You may request deletion at any time.

7. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact us at: callscoachai@gmail.com
